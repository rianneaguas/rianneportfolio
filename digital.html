stellar explorers

board game design

2019 Technology Student Association (TSA) National Competition Finalist for "Board Game Design"

I fully created and designed the game board and cover for my team's submission at the 2019 National TSA Competition.

Using Procreate and Photoshop, I used a myriad of brushes and digital mediums to complete this project.

This design placed regionally in CA, allowing my team to compete with it at the national competition.

the pen

newspaper logo and heading design

Design for Palos Verdes Peninsula HS's award-winning newspaper.

Serving as the editor-in-chief of The Pen, I took on the responsibility of designing our newspaper logo for the coming year. Using calligraphy brushes on Procreate, I tested many prototypes for the logo before coming up with the final version.

The final version is used on all issues from the 2019-20 school year. During this year, The Pen continued to win awards for both design and content.
